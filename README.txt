Functionality of the perfusion menu
===================================

Pixel-based semi-quantitative analysis. 

Pixel-based model-free deconvolution analysis.

Pixel-based modified Tofts analysis.

ROI-based fitting of exchange-models (plasma-interstitium).
 
ROI-based fitting of kidney models.

ROI-based fitting of liver models.
